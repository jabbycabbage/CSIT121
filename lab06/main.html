Lab 6: UI/UX Evaluation

The Website

https://www.nexusmods.com/

Nexus Mods

This website's target audience is the modding community of the game Skyrim by Bethesda.

Review of the Website

This site is organized so that users can get where they need to immediately, whether it be to navigate to one of the community's games, or to log in to their account, or to access the community and support resources. The good thing about this website is that from the starting/home page, it takes only 1 click to get where you really need to be.

In terms of the CRAP Design Principle, this site uses the proximity principle very well. In the list of mods, it puts all of the games next to each other, where the main focus is. It would have been very confusing if some games were at one place and other games were at a completely different place.

Audit Score

Effectiveness

The website is effective in the sense that it gets its goal accomplished, giving users an easy time navigating through the website's mods and games to add to their collection.

Efficiency

The website, however, is not very efficient. With all of the ads and cookies in place, it can take quite long to complete user requests and clicks.

Engagement

The platform is pleasant to use. The user gets what they need and can navigate through where they want to go very easily. But sometimes the ads and cookies can be a hindrance.

One Recommendation

One recommendation I have for the website is to get rid of all the ads. It ruins the first principle of CRAP, which is contrast. The website should be easy looking on the eyes. With all these ads, it can be an eyesore for some.
